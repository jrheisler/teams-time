Using Teams Time

Monitor teammates across time zones, add new collaborators, and export or import rosters when your lineup changes.

  • Use the Overview tab to keep track of the current time and roster.
  • Add teammates with notes and time zones from the Add teammate tab.
  • Import and export rosters to stay in sync with your team.

Everything runs locally in your browser so you can keep sensitive scheduling details in your control.
